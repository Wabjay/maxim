According to Data from United Nations, there are
Over 10 million African migrants in the US & Europe

These are people excluded from the credit financial system on a global level. This creates financial disparity, social hardship, and socio-economic exclusion for African immigrants

Maxim is working to address theses challenges and barriers faced by African immigrants in accessing credit and financial services by building a universal, borderless credit rail that can help them take full advantage of credit-bound opportunities on a global scale.
